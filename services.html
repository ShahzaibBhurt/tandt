Our Services

T&T Express Shipping provides exceptional shipping services that you can be sure to rely on. Services are provided from state-to-state and worldwide. Our varying services include:

  • Weekly Sailing:
    Please note this depends on cargo load.
  • Port-to-Port Service:
    We'll ship your cargo to the port for pickup.
  • Door-to-Door Service (Trinidad only):
    We'll pickup, clear and deliver your cargo to your door. Full service package.
  • Pack & Crate:
    We'll ensure that your cargo is properly wrapped, crated and strapped for shipping
  • Pickup & Delivery:
    residential and in-store pickup
  • Pack Furniture & Appliances
  • Handle & Load Commercial Cargo Trucks and Containers
  • Ship Vehicles or Furniture, drums, LCL, FCL, FTL or LTL freight
  • Off-site Estimates:
    free for Brooklyn, Queens only.
