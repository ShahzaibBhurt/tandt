Company Profile

T&T Express Shipping, LLC is your shipping and freight forwarder, providing "better service, better shipping" to the Caribbean and worldwide. We have presence in New York, New Jersey, Boston, Maryland, Pennsylvania and Florida.

Boasting over 20 years experience in shipping, we are the #1 shipper to Trinidad & Tobago. At T&T Express Shipping LLC, we know that your freight is very important, and we work hard to ensure our clients' belongings are packed, shipped and delivered with great care and efficiency.

You can rely on us for your freight shipping by land, sea, or air. We ship and export both domestically and internationally to the following countries: Trinidad & Tobago, Dominica, St. Lucia, Antigua, Jamaica, St. Maarten, Guyana, Haiti, US Virgin Islands, St. Vincent, South Central America. We also provide freight shipping services within the continental United States.

We offer freight shipping services at competitive and reasonable rates and carry a variety of shipping containers for your fragile cargo. We ship large open shipments, commercial freight, and we can even build crates (large or small), for fragile or sentimental items. Whether you want to ship vehicles or furniture, drums, LCL, FCL, FTL or LTL freight - we are the #1 choice.
